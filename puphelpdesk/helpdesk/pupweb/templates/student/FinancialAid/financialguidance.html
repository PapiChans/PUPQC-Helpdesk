Financial Aid Guidance

Welcome to our school's Financial Aid Guidance section. We are here to assist you in understanding and navigating the financial aid application process, specifically tailored to our scholarship criteria.

Step 1: Determine Eligibility

Our school offers scholarships based on your previous school's Grade Weighted Average (GWA) and your performance on the entrance exam. Ensure you meet these criteria before proceeding.

  • Grade Weighted Average (GWA) of 87 or higher in Math.
  • Grade Weighted Average (GWA) of 86 or higher in English.
  • Grade Weighted Average (GWA) of 89 or higher in Science.
  • Pass the school's entrance exam with a minimum qualifying score.

Step 2: Prepare Your Documents

Gather the necessary documents, including your academic transcripts from your previous school and any required entrance exam results or certificates.

  • Copy of your Grade 11 Card.
  • Copy of your Grade 10 Card.

Step 3: Fill up this form

Fill up the application form. Click Here

Step 4: Submit Your Application

Follow our application instructions to submit your scholarship application, including the required documents, by the specified deadline.

Step 5: Await Notification

After submitting your application, we will review it and notify you of the scholarship decision. Be sure to check your email and our website for updates.

Step 6: Scholarship Acceptance

If you are awarded a scholarship, follow the provided instructions to accept it and complete any additional requirements.
