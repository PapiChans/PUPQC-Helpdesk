Services

Services offered by myPUPQC helpdesk. Log In to continue

Financial Aid and Scholarships

This initiative provides guidance for students in navigating the financial aid application process and offers support in applying for scholarships.

Student Support and Counseling

This program offers counseling services for mental health support, academic advising referrals for course planning, and resources to improve study skills and academic success.

Housing and Accommodation

This program offers information on both on-campus and off-campus housing options and provides assistance in finding suitable off-campus housing and related services.

Health and Wellness Support

This service connects students with campus health services and medical clinics while also offering information on health insurance options and coverage.

Career Services and Employment

This service guides students to career counseling and job search resources, as well as provides information on available job and internship opportunities.

Student IDs and Access Cards

This service assists students in obtaining and replacing student identification cards and provides support for access cards used for campus facilities and services.

Student Government and Involvement

This service provides information about student government elections, roles, and participation opportunities and assists students in joining campus clubs and organizations.

Transportation and Parking

This service offers details about public transportation options and schedules, as well as assistance with campus parking permits and regulations.

Lost and Found Services

Lost Item Reporting: Provides a process for reporting and recovering lost items on campus. You can also find your missing items here.
