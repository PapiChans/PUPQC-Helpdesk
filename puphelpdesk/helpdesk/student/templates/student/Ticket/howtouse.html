How to Use Ticket Support?

In this page, you'll find guidance on how to utilize our Help Desk system tailored specifically for students. Our platform is dedicated to addressing your academic needs, resolving student concerns, and providing guides for the application process. Whether you're seeking assistance with course registration, campus facilities, or navigating the university's application procedures, our helpdesk system is here to support you every step of the way.

Creating a Ticket

Reminder: User can create 1 Ticket at the time, If the user wants to create another ticket, it needs to close the current Ticket first.

1. To create a ticket you need to go first on the Ticket page on the navigation bar.

(Navigation bar for Desktop / Laptop)

(Navigation bar for Mobile / Tablet)

2. Click the 'Create a Ticket' button as shown below.

3. Fill-out the required fields in the form once the form modal appears.

Subject - Specify the main concern about your ticket, Ex. Academic Concerns, Service guide or Campus Assistance.

4. Once you've done, submit the ticket by clicking 'Submit a Ticket' button.

Warning: This prompt will appear, indicating that your ticket cannot be edited once created.

To continue, click the 'Submit' Button from the prompt. Your ticket is created and you will proceed to the Ticket Conversation page.

Responding to a Ticket

(Assuming that your ticket has replied by the Administrator and that status should be 'Replied'.)

1. Go to the Ticket Page from the Navigation Bar

(Navigation bar for Desktop / Laptop)

(Navigation bar for Mobile / Tablet)

2. Choose a Ticket and click 'View' Button to go in Ticket Conversation page.

3. Create your message on the 'Your Response' field.

(Optional: You may attach a file for your Ticket message.)

4. Submit the message by clicking the 'Submit' button

(Optional: You may attach a file for your Ticket message.)

Warning: This prompt will appear, indicating that your message cannot be edited once submitted.

To continue, click the 'Submit' Button from the prompt. Your message is sent and wait for the Administrator to reply as soon as possible.

Ticket Status

Below are the various ticket statuses along with their meanings:

Status Information

Open - The User create a ticket and sent a message or create a response to the ticket, waiting for the Administrator to reply.

Replied - The Administrator send a response to the ticket, waiting for the User to reply.

Closed - The Ticket has been closed and it can't be sent response anymore.
